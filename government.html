Structure of Government

The NCN combined institutions inherited from Noble with councils created for newer member nations. Noblecrest remained the permanent capital and held the central archives, treasury, and radio network.

InstitutionFunction
Confederate ParliamentLegislation and provincial representation
PresidiumExecutive coordination and emergency decrees
Provincial CouncilMember-state disputes and regional policy
High Civic CourtCharter interpretation and administrative review

Emergency Continuity

Public law authorized relocation of records during invasion or civic collapse. No public statute described a permanent shadow government. Later “continuity committees” therefore lacked clear constitutional standing.

Noblecrest: Seat of the Northern Confederation

NOBLECREST CIVIC DISTRICT
Image unavailable in archive capture

Noblecrest served as the governmental, rail, broadcasting, and ceremonial heart of the NCN. Its ministries were arranged around Crest Avenue, while the old Noble Parliament occupied the northern end of the civic square.

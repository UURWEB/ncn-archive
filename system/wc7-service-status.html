PROJECT OVERVIEW

White Corridor was never a single bunker. It was a distributed chain of ordinary government and civilian computers carrying fragments of the same national archive.

No single terminal held enough information to claim executive authority. Seals, treasury keys, identity ledgers, and final orders were separated so that capture of one node could not recreate the state.
